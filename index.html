Biography

I graduated from the department of Computer Science (CAD&CG State Key Lab) at Zhejiang University with a master degree in 2022, advised by Prof. Xiaowei Zhou. Before that, I obtained my bachelor degree at Zhejiang University in 2019. My research focuses on character animation and human reconstruction (Demos).
