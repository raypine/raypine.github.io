Short Bio

I graduated from the Department of Computer Science at Zhejiang University with a master degree in 2022, advised by Prof. Xiaowei Zhou. Before that, I obtained my bachelor degree in Information Engineering (major) and Chu Kochen ACEE (minor) at Zhejiang University in 2019. My research focuses on human motion capture and reconstrcution.
